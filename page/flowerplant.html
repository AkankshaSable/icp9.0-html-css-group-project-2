Flower plants..

Flower plants are a popular choice for adding some color and fragrance to your garden or home. They can range from delicate perennials to bold annuals, and they can bloom in a wide range of colors and shapes. Flower plants can attract pollinators and create a beautiful focal point in any space.Flowers are the reproductive part of flowering plants, also known as angiosperms. They are the most prominent part of the plant and can come in many different colors, sizes, shapes, and arrangements. Flowers have both male and female reproductive organs, the stamen and pistil, which form male and female gametes (pollen and ovule). After fertilization, the gametes fuse to form a zygote and the ovary develops into fruit. Flowers can be unisexual, having only one organ, or bisexual, having both.
